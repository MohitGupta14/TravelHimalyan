## **Key Trekking Calendar**

### **Himachal Pradesh Treks**
- **March-June & September-November**: Triund, Kheerganga, Prashar Lake, Bhrigu Lake
- **May-October**: Hampta Pass, Beas Kund, Pin Parvati Pass
- **June-September**: Spiti Valley circuits, Chandratal Lake
- **December-February**: Winter treks with proper gear

### **Uttarakhand Treks**
- **April-June & September-November**: Valley of Flowers, Roopkund, Kedarnath
- **May-June & September-October**: Gaumukh Tapovan, Har Ki Dun
- **December-March**: Brahmatal, Kedarkantha (winter treks)
- **March-May & October-November**: Nag Tibba, Chopta-Tungnath

## **Special Considerations**

### **Monsoon Period (July-August)**
- **Avoid**: High altitude treks, landslide-prone areas
- **Best Options**: Spiti Valley (rain shadow), indoor cultural activities
- **Prepare for**: Road closures, limited connectivity

### **Winter Period (December-February)**
- **Best for**: Snow activities, winter photography, cozy retreats
- **Prepare for**: Heavy woolens, possible road blocks
- **Ideal**: Lower altitude destinations, established hill stations

### **Peak Seasons**
- **May-June**: Summer rush, book accommodations early
- **October**: Perfect weather, highest demand
- **December-January**: Winter tourism peak in snow destinations

## **Pro Tips**
1. **Book in advance** during peak seasons (May-June, October, December-January)
2. **Check road conditions** before traveling to high altitude areas
3. **Carry proper gear** for altitude and weather changes
4. **Respect local customs** and environmental guidelines
5. **Stay updated** on weather forecasts and trek permissions
